Max Super Speciality Hospital, Bathinda

The 200+ bed Max Super Speciality Hospital, Bhatinda is a unit of Hometrail Buildtech Pvt. Ltd. It is known for its expertise in Neurosciences, Orthopaedics, Cardiac Sciences, Cancer Care, Obstetrics and Gynaecology, Minimal Access, Metabolic, and Bariatric Surgery.Additionally, it has a Chemo Day Care Unit, Surgical Intensive Care Unit (SICU), Neonatal Intensive Care Unit (NICU), Paediatric Intensive Care Unit (PICU), a Medical Intensive Care Unit (MICU), as well as a Cath Lab. With 31 ICU beds and 4 high-end modular Operation Theatres, Max Super Speciality Hospital, Bathinda is home to the latest medical technologies. And under the guidance of over 52 leading doctors and medical experts, and over 140 nurses, the hospital is committed to providing world-class medical care to everyone. With over 30 clinical specialities, Max Super Specialty Hospital, Bathinda has been hailed for its superior infrastructure as well as high health and safety standards
